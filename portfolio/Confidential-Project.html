Confidential Project

June 2015 — September 2015

I designed all of the wireframes for a full-fledged responsive website where the final document was over 125 pages long. There were at least three wireframes for every one webpage that clearly illustrated its design for three different size viewports. In addition, each wireframe thoroughly described its functionality.

Unfortunately, I cannot publicly talk about the details of this project until the website is live.

For: Publicis North America

Role: Freelance Experience Designer/User Experience Design Intern

Type: Website

Tools: OmniGraffle
